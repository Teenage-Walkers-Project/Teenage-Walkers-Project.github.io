Mar 10th, 2014

Introduction of Tools for Communication Exchange

[Home Page]

This tool has a feature that you can get same contents from everywhere. We can use it for Self-Introduction, Popular trend, School and Regional Introduction. Comparing with each homepage among participating students will promote opportunities for mutual cultural understanding and cultivate the basis of human relationship. If the home pages contain private information, it should be protected under identification and password system.

[Bulletin Board]

This tool which has two useful functions as E-mail and Mailing List will be managed easily in technology matter once it is set up. The functions will work well for group and individual activities among participating students and produce a place to exchange opinions and ideas between them. They can use this tool freely with identification and password system to protect their personal information. Participating schools and the teachers should check the bulletin board regularly and cope with misusing the bulletin board or inadequate messages contributed on it to lead miscommunication.

[Live Video Conference]

Live video conference system software like CU-SeeMe or Netmeeting can send and receive real-time processing voices, movies and words crossing over boarders. This tool will realize to communicate among students watching their faces with each other. And it will create opportunities for students to exchange latest information related to regional and live cultural issues which mass media usually never deal.

[Home Stay]

It is important for students to set up a chance to meet each other with face to face revel to increase their relationship built on the Internet. Physical feeling or contact like a scent which is never transferred through the Internet should be shared through the direct meeting. It will advance to learn cultural diversity and understanding and co-existence among different cultures.
